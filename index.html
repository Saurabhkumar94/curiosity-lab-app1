Module 2 - LED Circuit Challenge

Questions 6-10

Before answering, complete Game 2 with your kit! Build the CR2032 + LED circuit using copper tape. Record your hypothesis BEFORE connecting, then test it.

LED Circuit Reminder:
CR2032 Coin Cell (3V) FLAT FACE = (+) positive RIM EDGE = (-) negative Copper tape (+) --> [LONG LEG] LED [SHORT LEG] <-- Copper tape (-) Result: LED GLOWS = Hypothesis CONFIRMED!
Remember: LONGER leg of LED = positive (+). SHORTER leg = negative (-). CR2032 is 3V and completely safe. Reverse polarity will NOT damage the LED - it simply will not light.
